Welcome to Lima smart Agriculture Optimisation System by Tafara Mundere

LimaSmart is a system that performs the following functions:

  • Advice farmers on potential diseases in real time and help them with methods of preparation
  • Calculates the risk of a disease happening in real time thereby assisting financial institutions; government; insurance companies and other agriculture stakeholders in decision making and risk mitigation

Problems

  • Farmer can’t easily access funding
  • Farmer can’t access affordable customized insurance package
  • Farmer is not able to proactively react to pests and disease outbreaks
  • Agricultural initiatives e.g. command agriculture difficulty in dealing with risk associated with pests and diseases

Solutions

  • Farmer get advice on potential risk associated with current weather and what they should have to prepare
  • Financial institutions (banks and microfinance) are able to quantify risk and mitigate it thereby unlocking funding opportunities for farmers
  • With risk now being measurable and predicted quantitatively affordable and customized insurance packages available for farmers
  • Agriculture programs e.g. Command Agriculture now being able to monitor and prevent  loss as a result of diseases and pests

Expected impact

  • Increase funding for farmers
  • Increase in the uptake of insurance by farmers
  • Reduce loss to pests and disease
  • Creation of ecosystem of agriculture stakeholders [Farmers; Banks; Agro-dealers; Insurance Companies; Millers Government]
